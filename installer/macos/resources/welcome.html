Vex Atlas

Version control for IFC and Revit models. This installer sets up the command-line client and the local pairing daemon so your CAD plugins can hand models to Vex Atlas without you typing anything.

  • Installs two binaries to /usr/local/bin — vex and vex-bridge.
  • Registers a launchd agent so vex-bridge starts at every login. No login items to manage.
  • Stays on your machine. The daemon binds 127.0.0.1:7878 only — nothing on your network can reach it.
  • Opens your browser when the installer finishes. Approve this device once and you're paired.
