Vex Atlas early access install

This installer sets up the local vex CLI, the vex-bridge daemon, and the Revit add-in used by the ribbon commands. Restart Revit after install; the vex-bridge panel appears on the Add-Ins tab.

Unsigned early access build: Windows may show SmartScreen because the installer is not code-signed yet. Click More info, then Run anyway. This is expected for the early access release.

Connect your Architur account

  1. Open Revit and click Pair this device on the vex-bridge ribbon panel.
  2. Your browser opens to studio.planmorph.software/pair.
  3. Sign in. If you do not have an account, choose Create account; free individual accounts do not require a card.
  4. Approve the device. The private key stays on this machine; Architur receives only the public key.
  5. Back in Revit, click Push to architur, choose your project, and push the current model.
